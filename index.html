A Home for Green Energy

Making the best decisions for your home can be difficult with the plethora of options available. If you are interested in making a smaller impact on the environment, this site can help you find the ways

Windows

If you are in the market for new windows, be sure to look at Energy Star Certified products while shopping. In addition to saving you a possible $500 in heating and cooling costs, residents of the United States are eligible for a $1,500 tax credit on qualifying installations. Please speak to a tax professional for more information.

Additionally, try using shades or curtains to block the summer sun instead of turning up the air conditioning. Planting a deciduous tree near your window is also a cheap and easy way to lower energy costs. Leafy in the summer, it naturally keeps the heat away, but bare in the winter, this type of tree lets the light through to warm your home.
